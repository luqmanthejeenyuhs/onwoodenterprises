Company Overview

Founded in 2006 in Kilifi County, Onwood Enterprises and General Contractors Ltd began as a sole proprietorship focused on building construction and civil works.

The company transitioned into a private limited entity in 2023 under the Companies Act of Kenya.

We provide end-to-end construction solutions including structural building, civil works, renovations, and plumbing installations across the Coast Region.
